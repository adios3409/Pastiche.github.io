Theme Based Playing Cards

The theme is chose for my cards is DPR Ian's songs from the album Moodswings In To Order. So my each card represents a song from the album.
Concept Note: I have used red and black because that is the main color that is seen in the album cover. I have used Ibis Paint X to create the Illustrations. The King card represents the song Ballroom Extravaganza.The Queen card represents the song Winterfall. The Jack card represents the song Seraph and the Joker cards represents the song Mr.Insanity.
