My font is inspired by the shape of a boomerang. The angular triangle is the common part in every letter. Brand name is Bhavra which means Buzzing sound of joy and celebration ,associated with sweets

Concept Note: The design is anchored in a bold color palette of golden yellow and deep purple. Yellow, symbolic of sweetness, warmth, and festivity, instantly evokes joy and indulgence. The contrasting purple adds a touch of luxury, modernity, and prestige, elevating the brand as a premium yet accessible treat for every occasion. The geometric typography of Bhavra is dynamic and striking, drawing inspiration from traditional Indian motifs and angular forms. It gives a sense of movement and energy, appealing to a youthful audience that values uniqueness and style. The intricate border patterns surrounding the brand name, inspired by tribal and regional art forms, create a subtle nod to Indian heritage while maintaining a clean, contemporary aesthetic. The tagline, "The Medal of Every Bite," reinforces Bhavra's promise: every sweet is crafted to deliver a celebratory experience, rewarding consumers with flavor, joy, and nostalgia. This modern twist on traditional Indian sweets positions Bhavra as the go-to brand for those who seek a perfect balance of culture and trend.
