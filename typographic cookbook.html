Typographic Cookbook

The topic I chose for the cookbook is : Midnight Cravings. The other options i had were Comfort foods, 10 min recipes and Healthy Soup recipes. In the end I chose Midnight Cravings as my topic because I am someone who stays up late working and get random cravings but don’t know what to cook. So the target audience is chose is Teenagers and People who work till late.

Concept Note: For the cookbook, I tried to chose snacks that are quick, easy to prepare, and use common ingredients. The recipes chosen like Egg Muffins and Peanut Butter Cookies are beginner-friendly and highly customizable. I mainly focused on recipes that cater to people’s love for fusion and trendy dishes. These reflect midnight snack culture with easy-to-make, flavorful, and relatable options. The recipes are chosen from trending Instagram reels, you tube cooking channels and online blogs. Late-night workers prefer easy-to-make options for a satisfying yet efficient snack, especially teenagers often crave fun and trendy dishes. The target audience I chose resonates with the preference of vibrant layouts, playful language, snackable portions and minimal effort recipes. I chose the font Pincoyablack Black because it seemed to be modern, playful, and adaptable to both energetic and professional tones. Because my font is so heavy, for the recipe page i chose to keep it simple.
