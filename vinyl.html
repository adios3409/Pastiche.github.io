ALBUM CHOSED: MASTER OF PUPPETS BY METALLICA

Concept Note: For my album concept, I wanted to create something that evokes a sense of eeriness and mystery, perfectly capturing the unsettling themes of control and manipulation. The album, titled Master of Puppets, visualizes a dark and enigmatic Master who exerts control over people, symbolizing their lack of autonomy. Instead of showing the people directly, they are depicted as lifeless masks, hanging as if suspended by invisible strings. The color palette for the album is dominated by shades of dark green and black, giving it a shadowy, otherworldly aesthetic that enhances the overall atmosphere. The backdrop for this vision is a desolate graveyard, filled with crumbling tombstones and overgrown vegetation, shrouded in a misty haze. This setting reinforces the haunting and oppressive tone of the album while hinting at themes of mortality and despair. The imagery captures the essence of a controlling force looming over powerless individuals, blending the sinister with the symbolic to create a striking and memorable visual representation for the album.
